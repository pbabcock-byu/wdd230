Extended Stay Options

Temple Inn and Suites offers a clean, fully furnished studio at an affordable price. In addition to the typical hotel furniture, our rooms include a full size refrigerator/freezer, microwave, two-burner stovetop and ample cabinet space. Safety is a priority at our hotels with no exterior room doors, eight cameras and a well-lit parking lot. Our interior-corridor entrances can only be accessed with a valid key card offering a restful, pleasant hotel experience. Our friendly staff is available 24/7 for anything from items needed for your room to directions to a nearby tourist attraction. Our staff takes great pride in the cleanliness of the rooms and hotel.
