Design Principles

Peter J. Babcock

Repetition

Amazon

Amazon.com

I picked the Amazon site as it seems to me they are using the repetition principle.
As you scroll down there are alot of different secitons, each one trying to entice you to build a certain category.
Each category shows 4 images. They are the top items Amazon think will entice you to buy that category.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple.com

I picked Apple for this princple as they have a very white and clean design.
I feel they are trying to show/tell you they are really classy and have premium range of products.

Contrast

Boost Mobile

boostmobile.com

The reason i chose this site is because of the colors they use to stand out.
They use the color orange all over there site and its the main color of their logo. They are a value providor and almost everything in orange is trying to shout value or important info.
